ABOUT US

Founded in 1998, we started service with 6 restaurants and two employees. SUXES Hospitality Solutions Pvt. Ltd. has taken over the entire Business – Management & Operations – in August, 2013.

Since then, we have grown in both – size and service offerings, becoming well known in Pune, Pimpri-Chinchwad and NOW Navi-Mumbai, as a quality Home Delivery service provider.

Nearly 75% of our business comes from existing registered customers.

Today we offer menus from over 200 restaurants / outlets with new ones being registered regularly.

We have a group of well-trained and experienced STAFF and are known for our timely and efficient delivery.

We hope you will continue to use our services and recommend them to all your friends.
